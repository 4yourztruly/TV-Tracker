# TV Tracker

Never lose your place in a show again. TV Tracker is a mobile-first web app for keeping track of exactly what episode you're on across every show and anime you watch — add it to your home screen and it feels just like a native app. Your data stays on your device, or syncs to your own Google Drive if you want it backed up and available on every device.

**[Try it now →](https://4yourztruly.github.io/TV-Tracker/)**

### Automatic progress tracking

TV Tracker keeps count of exactly how many episodes you have left on every show, and flags the moments that matter — series finales, season finales, premieres — so you always know what's next.
